Hello

I'm a writer and designer living and working in Louisville, Kentucky. My beautiful wife and I herd three crazy cats and watch Netflix every so often. Nice to meet you.

What I do

I find that the title of Web Generalist best applies to what I do for a living.

During the last 17 years, I’ve worked as a designer, front-end developer, project manager, and content strategist. The decision not to specialize in one discipline was a conscious one. With a wider range of skills, I'm able focus on a bigger picture.

By Day

In the few months I've worked there as a Visual Designer, I've learned quite a bit about iterative design centered around user value. Recently, I shifted to a Product Management position, which provides several new challenges and opportunities.

By Night
